The Art of Bristol

Welcome!

This page is dedicated to Banksy and all of the fabulous street art around Bristol since as Bristol residents, we see art all of the time! But we want to have the ability to find a particular piece or find out more about the art we come across. If you enjoy finding all of the art around where you live, or you're visiting Bristol and you want to see as much art as possible during your time here then this is the site for you!

Here you will find all sorts of useful information for your explorations! On the Maps page, you can check your area and see what art is close and there is even a number of trails maps for you to follow, perfect if you are time constrained but what to see everything! On the Directory page, you can find out all sort of information about your favorite pieces or find a new piece that looks interesting to go and find.

Keep an eye on our News page to stay up-to-date with all the latest news regarding art in Bristol, including when new art is spotted!

Go have an explore!. Both this site and Bristol have plenty of fun tibits for you to find!

If you have any knowledge of art we haven't featured or suggestions for us, please use the contact form on the Directory page!
